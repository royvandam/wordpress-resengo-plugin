Resengo - Manage reservations, communicate and sell gift cards online. Resengo has a solution for every entrepreneur.

== Description ==
Resengo is an innovative digital platform that offers the most comprehensive online reservation and communication solutions on the market. Resengo empowers the hospitality, beauty and wellness sector and is one of the largest players on the Belgian and Dutch market. On a monthly basis, the company processes some 1.5 million reservations and more than 10 000 gift vouchers.

This plugin enables you to easily install the Resengo widget on your Wordpress website. The Resengo widget allows visitors to book a reservation, buy a gift voucher or order food.

== Installation ==
1. Upload the resengo-widget folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
2. Activate the Resengo Widget Plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Add Company ID and select Language in plugin settings under Settings menu.
